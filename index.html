WE ARE ANTI-LYL.

ABOUT THE ANTI-LYL FAMILY

Our goal at Anti-Lyl is to defeat the mighty villain - Lyl. We have been searching for lyl for nearly a year to no avail. We have been recruiting new team members for the purpose of finding him quicker and killing him.

WHO IS LYL?

Lyl is the ultimate traitor to all that is good in this world. He has been ravaging the "Rainbow Six: Siege" community with inhumane toxicity. He can be easily recognized by his poor skill in the video game "Rainbow Six: Siege" and by his signature voice that resembles that of an old man on ecstacy.

WHERE IS LYL?

Lyl's location has been estimated to be in Scotland. Even if you do not live in Scotland, you can do your part in killing Lyl by joining a match of "Rainbow Six: Siege" and asking all of the players in the lobby if they love Lyl. If they say yes, prepare to unleash hellfire onto them.
